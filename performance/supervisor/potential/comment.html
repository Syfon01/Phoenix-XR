Qualitative & Potential Assessments

This is used in leadership skills assessment, career and succession planning and mapping your development needs. Assess and view ratings.

You are required to complete your self-assessment on both Qualitative and Potential Assessment; to assess your Supervisor, Subordinates, and Peers on their Competence, Commitment and Contribution to the growth of the organisation as well as their Learning Agility and our Core Values.

All peers rating will remain confidential and will not be disclosed to any member of your peers. Rate only peers who you have worked with directly. Your assessment must be honest and transparent.
